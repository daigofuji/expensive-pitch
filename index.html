The Boston Red Sox signed David Price to the record contract of average $31million a year this off season. With his 3,388 pitches thrown by him last year, that comes out to be over $9,000 a pitch. Yikes, right? That got me thinking, who was the most economical and who's pitch was the most expensive? 2016 Basebal Hack Day project. See other submissions here!
